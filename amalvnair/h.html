Major Soil Groups of Tamil Nadu

Red Soils (62%)

Predominant across the state. Rich in Iron/Alum oxides but low in Nitrogen. Perfect for Groundnuts & Millets.

Black Soils (12%)

Also known as 'Regur'. High clay content and moisture retention. Found in Coimbatore & Madurai. Best for Cotton.

Alluvial Soils (7%)

Highly fertile silt deposits along river beds like Cauvery. Ideal for Paddy & Sugarcane.
